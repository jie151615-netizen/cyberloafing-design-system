**SectionHeader** — the bilingual masthead that opens every section: an ALL-CAPS English display title with a red Chinese subtitle under it.

```jsx
<SectionHeader title="Cyberloafing" subtitle="摸鱼" />
<SectionHeader title="Snack Time!" subtitle="零食" size="h1" color="var(--red)" subtitleColor="var(--ink)" />
```

Use `size="display"` for full section openers, `size="h1"` for individual spreads. On coral or navy fields pass `color="var(--paper)"`. Keep the EN/中文 pairing — it's the core brand cue.
