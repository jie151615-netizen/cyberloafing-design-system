**StampStrip** — tracked-out stamp text repeated as margin texture ("WORKING WORKING…", "NOT IN CYBERLOAFING").

```jsx
<StampStrip text="WORKING" repeat={8} direction="vertical" />
<StampStrip text="NOT IN CYBERLOAFING" direction="horizontal" color="var(--coral)" />
```

It's decorative texture (aria-hidden). Keep it faint (ink tint) unless it's a deliberate accent band, then use coral/red.
